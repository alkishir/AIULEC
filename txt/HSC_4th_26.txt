Title: The child and the dog This is the day when Ali was walking in the garden, he found a dog sleeping homeless, and Ali loved the dog very much and wished that he had a dog for along time, but he was sad for the dog, so he took some wood and took a special house for him to rest in, He used to make it for him with love, and when he finished ,he was happy, and he took the dog to sleep and rest in it.

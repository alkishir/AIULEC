Title: My Favourt Book My favourite book “I Missed Prayr” by Dr Ayed Al-Qarani, is one of the books that has a deep spiritual nature, as it deals with the topic of the important of prayer in the life of muslim and it’s impact on the soul through it, the other present personal experiences about people who lost prayer and how this afficted their lives. The book calls for a return to Allah and attention to prayer as a basic pillar in the life of the believer, and encourges a sense of reverence and communication with Allah at all time. “I Missed Prayr” is not just a book about prayer, but rather a call for reflection and positive change in the life of the individual and society.

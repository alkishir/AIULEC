Diaries are important in keeping memories , whether good or bad . Keeping good memories is important for moments of sadness . You can always go back in time and read your exact feelings about these moments . This will make you realize how happy your life is , and how life has its ups and downs . It also makes you realize that your life isnt as bad as you think in moments of grief . When it comes to bad memories , it 's also important to keep them and write them down . Reading through your bad times shows you what youve accomplished . These memories good or bad , give you strength and a better understanding of the person youve become .
